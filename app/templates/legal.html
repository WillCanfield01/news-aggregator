PatchPal is a Slack app by THE REAL ROUNDUP LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”). It posts a daily “Top 5 patches/CVEs” message into a Slack channel you choose. By installing the app or using /patchpal commands, you agree to these Terms and our Privacy Policy.

1) What PatchPal does

  • At a time you select (Mon–Fri), PatchPal posts up to 5 prioritized patch/CVE items with plain-English context and “do this now” actions.
  • It uses trusted public sources (e.g., CISA KEV, vendor advisories) plus EPSS/KEV signals. No end-user personal content is processed.

2) Slack permissions (scopes)

We request only the scopes needed to operate:

  • commands – receive /patchpal slash commands.
  • chat:write & chat:write.public – post messages.
  • channels:read & groups:read – resolve channel IDs when you run set-channel.
  • users:read – identify the user who configures the app so we can DM billing/trial notices.
