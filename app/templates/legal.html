PatchPal — Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-28

PatchPal is a Slack app by THE REAL ROUNDUP LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”). It posts a daily “Top 5 patches/CVEs” message into a Slack channel you choose. By installing the app or using /patchpal commands, you agree to these Terms and our Privacy Policy.

1) What PatchPal does

  • At a time you select (Mon–Fri), PatchPal posts up to 5 prioritized patch/CVE items with plain-English context and “do this now” actions.
  • It uses trusted public sources (e.g., CISA KEV, vendor advisories) plus EPSS/KEV signals. No Slack message history is read or stored.
  • Optional: If you enable AI summaries (PATCHPAL_AI_REWRITE=1), we may send publicly available advisory text (not your Slack content) to OpenAI to generate shorter summaries.

2) Slack permissions (scopes)

We request only the scopes needed to operate:

  • commands – receive /patchpal slash commands.
  • chat:write – post messages.
  • channels:read & groups:read – resolve channel IDs when you run set-channel.
  • im:write – open a DM with the configuring admin to send trial/billing notices.

3) Free trial, billing & cancellation

  • Each workspace gets a 14-day free trial.
  • Paid plan is $9/workspace/month via Stripe subscription. Taxes may apply.
  • Cancel anytime in the Stripe Customer Portal (/patchpal billing). Service remains through the paid period; no prorated refunds except where required by law.
  • If payment fails or the trial expires, posts pause until payment is updated or you re-subscribe.

4) Important disclaimers

Informational use only. PatchPal summarizes public vulnerability information; it does not replace your patch management, vulnerability scanning, or incident response processes. We do not guarantee completeness, timeliness, or fitness for a particular purpose. You are responsible for validating actions before deployment.

5) Acceptable use

  • Don’t use PatchPal to break laws, abuse the Slack API, spam, or probe our infrastructure.
  • Don’t post confidential or regulated data to PatchPal commands; we don’t need it.

6) Availability

We aim for reliable weekday posting but provide the service on an “AS IS” and “AS AVAILABLE” basis. We may pause service for maintenance or to address abuse/security events.

7) Privacy Policy

Data we receive/store

  • Workspace identifiers: team_id, workspace name.
  • Installation token: Slack bot token for your workspace (stored securely in our database; used only to post and check membership).
  • Posting settings: timezone, channel ID, daily post time, tone.
  • Minimal contact: a Slack user ID (the person who configured PatchPal) to send configuration or billing DMs.
  • Billing data via Stripe: customer ID, subscription ID, payment status (we never see card numbers).
  • De-dupe & logs: date-only post log; recent item keys (recent_posts) used to avoid repeats.

Data we do not store

  • We don’t store your Slack message history.
  • We don’t sell or share personal data for advertising.

How we use the data

  • Operate the app (post the Top 5 at your chosen time).
  • Handle trials, subscriptions, and receipts through Stripe.
  • Security, debugging, and abuse prevention (basic logs and metrics).

Retention & deletion

  • Configuration persists while the app is installed.
  • recent_posts entries include timestamps; items older than your retention window (default 5 days) are automatically ignored when selecting new posts. Rows may remain in the database until purged.
  • Delete workspace data: a workspace admin can email wc@therealroundup.com with the workspace team_id. We will delete the installation (token), workspace settings, and recent_posts records, typically within 7 days.

Sub-processors

  • Slack (workspace platform)
  • Stripe (payments & customer portal)
  • Render.com or equivalent (hosting/database)
  • OpenAI (summary rewriting, only if enabled; public advisory text only)

Security

  • Transport via TLS; database encryption at rest provided by our cloud provider.
  • Secrets stored as environment variables; least-privilege access.

International, GDPR/CCPA

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us and we’ll help. We are a processor for workspace config data; Stripe is a separate controller/processor for payments.
